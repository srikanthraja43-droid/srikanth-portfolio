OUR MISSION

Redefining PG Management.

PG360 was born out of a simple observation: managing a PG is unnecessarily chaotic. For years, PG owners have had to rely on registers, complicated spreadsheets, endless WhatsApp groups, and sheer willpower.

We believe that managing your property should be calm and entirely in your control. By blending intuitive design and automation, we created a dashboard that replaces the chaos, letting you focus on scaling your business and providing better service to your tenants.
