EVERYTHING IN ONE PLACE

Six tightly-built modules replace your registers, spreadsheets and a dozen WhatsApp groups.

Rooms & beds

Track every room, bed and floor with live vacant / partial / occupied status that updates as tenants move in and out.

Tenants & KYC

Onboard residents with Aadhaar, deposit and check-in date — each gets their own login automatically.

Rent & dues

Record rent with an electricity + food + other breakdown. See exactly who paid and who is due this month.

Food & mess

Weekly menus and per-meal opt in / out that flows straight into each tenant's monthly bill.

Complaints

Tenants raise issues with photos; you triage open → in-progress → resolved without the WhatsApp mess.

P&L & reports

Collected minus expenses, occupancy and turnover — exportable, so you always know your real profit.
